About the App

Fasting Manager (断食する？) combines intermittent fasting schedule management with Apple HealthKit data viewing — all in one app. Get notified when your fasting window starts and ends, and track your health metrics with clear charts.

iPhone HealthKit iOS 17+

Key Features

1. Fasting Timer

Choose from four popular intermittent fasting protocols and let the app manage your daily schedule automatically.

  • 16:8 — 16 hours fasting / 8 hours eating
  • 18:6 — 18 hours fasting / 6 hours eating
  • 12:12 — 12 hours fasting / 12 hours eating
  • OMAD — One Meal A Day (23 hours fasting)
  • Choose which days of the week to fast
  • Customize your meal start time
  • Status banner shows your current fasting / eating phase at a glance

2. Fasting Notifications

  • Local notifications remind you when fasting starts and ends

3. Health Data Viewer

Visualize your Apple HealthKit data with charts and detailed lists.

  • Supports body weight, blood pressure (systolic/diastolic), heart rate, and step count
  • Four time ranges: day, week, month, and year
  • List view shows raw numeric values with timestamps
  • Export data as CSV

4. Measurement Reminders

  • Get reminded to measure your blood pressure or body weight
  • Customize the reminder time and days of the week

5. Privacy First

  • All health data stays on your device — never sent to external servers
  • HealthKit access requires your explicit permission
  • Ad privacy settings via Google UMP (GDPR / ATT compliant)
